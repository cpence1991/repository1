About

My Name is Christopher D. Pence, and I am a web design student from Towson, Maryland.

I first entered college with the plan in mind to become a history teacher. I first attended the Community College of Baltimore County, gathering the basic prerequisite classes needed for all students, in order to focus purely on my major once I transferred to a university. I graduated with an Associate's Degree in General Studies. During my time at Salisbury University, I studied primarily in history and the teaching arts. Discovering that I felt uncomfortable as a classroom teacher, I finished my Bachelor's Degree in History. This degree unfortunately did not afford me much in the career world, and shortly after, I decided to pursue a Master's Degree. I am currently a student at Towson University, studying the Computer Sciences. I intend to graduate in 2020 with my Master's Degree in Computer Science.
