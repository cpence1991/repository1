Paul Stanley

Paul Stanley (born Stanley Bert Eisen; January 20, 1952) is an American musician, singer, songwriter and painter best known for being the rhythm guitarist and co-lead vocalist of the rock band Kiss. He is the writer or co-writer of many of the band's highest-charting hits. Stanley established The Starchild character for his Kiss persona. Hit Parader ranked him 18th on their list of Top 100 Metal Vocalists of All Time. Gibson.com Readers Poll also named him 13th on their list of Top 25 Frontmen. -Wikipedia
